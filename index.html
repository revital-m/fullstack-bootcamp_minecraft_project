You have 3 types of tools:

  • Pickaxe - for mining rocks.

  • Shovel - for digging dirt.

  • Axe - for cutting trees.

Clicking on a tool followed by clicking on a tile in the world will remove the tile, (If it is of the correct type) and add it to the inventory.

You can also click on the tile in the inventory and place it back in the world.

Enjoy!
